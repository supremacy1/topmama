About Us

TOPMAMA Pizza is a renowned pizza restaurant chain founded in 2020 by the director of Planet Restaurant & Bar, Ugorji Okorie in Port Harcourt, Rivers State. We pride ourselves on serving exceptional pizzas, fostering a culture of excellence, and building lasting relationships with our customers.

Our Story

As a family-owned Pizza Restaurant, TOPMAMA is driven by Ugorji's dedication to using only the freshest ingredients and innovative recipes. Word of our delicious pizzas spread rapidly, making us the go-to destination for pizza lovers in Port Harcourt. Our menu also features an assortment of delectable sides, including crispy chicken & chips, cheesy garlic bread, and gizzard & chips, perfect for complementing your pizza feast.
